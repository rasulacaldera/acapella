Who Are We

Who are we? Pulse of Life has built its foundation on creating awareness for kids’ cancer. As the sole entity that focuses on kids’ cancer, we accept the uncertainty of this non-communicable disease and make it our goal to ensure these little kids live their best life every day. In our capacity and through the generous donors who place their trust in us, we will keep the Pulse of the Lives of these kids steady. We at Pulse of Life immerse ourselves into supporting the day to day needs of these kids. We draw inspiration from the laughter and joy of our little warriors. The moments spent amidst childhood pleasures provide us the foothold to work towards providing them a chance at life.
